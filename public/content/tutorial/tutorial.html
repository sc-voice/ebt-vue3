Tutorial

Learn how to use EBT-Vue3

EBT-Vue3 lets you read and hear Early Buddhist Text (EBT) content from SuttaCentral.net. EBT-Vue3 shows content using one or more cards:

  • This page is on the Wiki card
  • Search cards let you search for EBT content
  • Sutta cards let you read/play EBT content

In this tutorial, you will learn how to:

Read/Play EBT content When the current card is a sutta, you can:
  • Click any segment
  • Play the selected segment
  • Play to the end of the sutta
Open a card

Open one or more cards to display content side-by-side. Each card has a card tab in the card tab bar. Click a card tab to show that card.

Close a card
  • Click the "-" to minimize a card.
  • Click the "X" to delete a card.
Search for EBT content To find EBT content:
  • Enter a phrase (e.g., "root of suffering")
  • Enter a sutta identifier (e.g., "thig1.1") in the search text field.
  • Click "Inspire Me" for a random search phrase

Scan the search results and click on any result to see the actual sutta.

Personalize settings

Settings are grouped into sections. Click each section and explore your options.
